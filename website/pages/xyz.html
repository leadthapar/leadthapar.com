Not every journey has a predefined path. It's through curiosity and looking at opportunities in new ways that we, at LEAD, have always mapped our path. Emerging with the idea of SCIMATICS, which consolidates Science and Mathematics, we at TIET started 5 years ago. Since then we have been igniting a spark in students to set new benchmarks in the technical world. To add emphasis to the purpose we were serving and hence to add oil to the spark we ignited, we commenced our 'mentor-learner' program, which endeavored to help each other exchange ideas and grow together.

We stood to justify what the abbreviation of LEAD stands for.

Learn Emerge Aspire Discover

Establishing this aim, we have always been embedding our best to come up with mentor-student Learning, from technical to designing and from research to innovation. Our students have been conquering the world by Emerging as true leaders. Our team has been Aspiring each day to achieve new success and affect more lives with our actions. Therefore, we Discover new ideas that can transform the professional, technical, and digital life of our team. in this backpacking journey, we joined hands with sponsors like Coding Blocks, GatsBy, Github, Stickermule, Townscript, and many more. We are shaping our couriers and new minds together with the team of 500+ members, who have concluded 50+ projects.
